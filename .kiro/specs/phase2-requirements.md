## 15. Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### 15.1 Technical Risks

#### High-Priority Risks
- [ ] **Performance Degradation**: Risk of slower performance with increased features
  - *Mitigation*: Comprehensive performance testing and optimization
- [ ] **Security Vulnerabilities**: Risk of security breaches with expanded attack surface
  - *Mitigation*: Regular security audits and penetration testing
- [ ] **Integration Complexity**: Risk of integration failures with third-party services
  - *Mitigation*: Robust error handling and fallback mechanisms
- [ ] **Data Migration Issues**: Risk of data loss during system upgrades
  - *Mitigation*: Comprehensive backup and rollback procedures

### 15.2 Business Risks

#### Business Risk Mitigation
- [ ] **User Adoption Challenges**: Risk of users not adopting new features
  - *Mitigation*: User training programs and gradual feature rollout
- [ ] **Increased Complexity**: Risk of system becoming too complex to manage
  - *Mitigation*: Comprehensive documentation and admin training
- [ ] **Cost Overruns**: Risk of development costs exceeding budget
  - *Mitigation*: Phased development approach with regular budget reviews
- [ ] **Competitive Pressure**: Risk of competitors launching similar features
  - *Mitigation*: Rapid development cycles and unique value propositions
